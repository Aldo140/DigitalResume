Tech LiftOff Program

Startup Strategy • Market Validation • Business Modeling

Program Overview

Tech LiftOff is a university-linked initiative that connects tech students with real-world mentors and startup founders. As part of the program, I collaborated with a multidisciplinary team to develop a full startup strategy and MVP concept — from ideation to product pitch.

My Role

  • Led product planning for a startup idea focused on user-driven data collection in real estate
  • Created competitive analysis frameworks and pitched to a panel of industry advisors
  • Mapped MVP requirements and timelines using Notion and Miro boards
  • Worked with a designer to mock up early-stage UI in Figma

What I Learned

This experience helped me improve my understanding of lean product development and investor pitching. I also gained insight into startup workflows and how to navigate ambiguity with limited resources.

Tech LiftOff showed me how to bridge the gap between business thinking and technical implementation. I left with a better sense of how real founders think and how to prioritize ruthlessly.
