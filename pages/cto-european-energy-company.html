⚡ CTO of European Energy Startup

During my time at the European Innovation Academy in Portugal, I stepped into the role of Chief Technology Officer (CTO) for a startup focused on clean energy solutions. Working alongside students, mentors from Google and Amazon, and business coaches, we developed a real product in just 15 days.

Responsibilities:

  • Led all tech development across frontend, backend, and deployment
  • Coordinated with a multinational, cross-functional team
  • Turned startup idea into a working MVP in two weeks

Key Wins:

  • Delivered a working energy dashboard with live user input
  • Presented product to international judges and investors
  • Built strong tech-business alignment through agile prototyping

This experience shaped how I approach startups, teamwork, and product delivery under pressure.
