Overview

CanRepay is a mobile-first student loan repayment planner built during CalgaryHacks to empower Canadian students to better manage their financial futures. The app helps users visualize debt, create repayment strategies, and track net worth in real time.

Key Features

  • Built with React Native and Expo for iOS, Android, and Web
  • Loan repayment calculators for federal/provincial programs
  • Real-time debt tracking and progress visualizations
  • Dynamic budgeting tips and alerts based on interest rates

Outcomes

  • Created a working MVP over a single weekend
  • Finalist in the fintech/education category
  • Presented the project to a panel of judges and local developers
